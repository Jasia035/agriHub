Crop Details

Wheat

Best for temperate climates. Requires well-drained loamy soil and moderate rainfall.

Rice (Paddy)

Grown in tropical regions. Needs high humidity and constant water supply (flooded fields).

Maize (Corn)

Highly versatile. Needs full sun and rich soil with good nitrogen levels.

Tomatoes

Warm-season crop. Requires support stakes and consistent watering to avoid fruit cracking.

Potatoes

Grown underground in loose, cool soil. Important to "hill" the soil as plants grow.

Coffee

High-altitude crop. Thrives in volcanic soil and partial shade (Shade-grown).

Pest Control

Biological Control

Introducing natural predators like ladybugs or lacewings to eat harmful aphids.

Organic Sprays

Using Neem oil or garlic-chili sprays as non-toxic deterrents for soft-bodied insects.

Fungal Management

Controlling powdery mildew and blight using copper-based or sulfur sprays.

Physical Removal

Manual removal of large pests like caterpillars or using sticky traps for flies.

Pheromone Traps

Luring specific male insects to traps to disrupt their breeding cycles.

Early Detection

Routine inspection of leaves and stems to catch infestations before they spread.
